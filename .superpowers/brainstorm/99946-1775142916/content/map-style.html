World Map Style: What look are you going for?

The map sits in the hero/stats section with death pulses. Click to select.

A. Outlined Continents

Thin stroke outlines of continents. Minimal, elegant. Red pulses stand out clearly against the empty shapes. Very lightweight.

B. Filled Continents (Low Opacity)

Solid continent shapes at very low opacity (6%). Subtle but gives the map more presence. Death pulses glow on top of the landmass shapes. Clean, modern.

C. Dotted Grid (Proper Clip-Path)

Uniform dot grid clipped to real continent shapes. Like the original concept but done properly — dots are evenly spaced, continent shapes from real geo data. Gives a "data visualization" feel that matches the ticker aesthetic.
