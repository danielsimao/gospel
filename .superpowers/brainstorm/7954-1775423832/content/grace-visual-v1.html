Proposed motion:
  • Heading + beat I: fade+rise in on scroll-into-view (existing IntersectionObserver pattern, duration 1.2s easeOut)
  • New beat on tap: fade+rise 8px, 0.5s easeOut. Previous "active" beat transitions to dim (opacity 1 → 0.32) over 0.4s.
  • Tap pill: pulses at 2.4s cycle until tapped. Disappears on beat IV (replaced by scripture + CTA).
  • Scripture + CTA on beat IV tap: reveal together, staggered (scripture at 0s, CTA at 0.4s).
Interaction target:
  • Whole tap pill is the button (large tap target, 48px min-height).
  • Not tappable: the beats themselves, to keep the "tap to continue" mechanic unambiguous.
Accessibility: button is a real <button>, respects prefers-reduced-motion (fades without y-translation).
